Amalfi Coast & Sorrento Weddings

The Amalfi Coast along with the Sorrentine peninsula is one of the most spectacular stretches of coastline in the world, no wonder it is the most popular area of Italy for weddings.  Choose from historic Sorrento, elegant Positano, beautiful Amalfi, or the serene hilltop village of Ravello for a wedding you will never forget.

Our wedding locations on the Amalfi Coast include historic museums, a cloisters, villas, gardens and luxury hotels with receptions at luxury hotels, beachfront restaurants and many locations with spectacular views.
